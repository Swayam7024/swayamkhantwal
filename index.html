About Me

IDENTITY CONFIRMED

I'm an engineering student on a mission to build machines that think, fly, and act autonomously. My work sits at the intersection of embedded systems, autonomous navigation, and drone engineering.

From flashing firmware on Pixhawk flight controllers to writing Python scripts that command drones over MAVProxy — I bridge the gap between hardware and software to create intelligent robotic systems.

Based in India, I'm passionate about pushing the frontier of what autonomous systems can do in the real world — from campus logistics to FPV racing and beyond.
